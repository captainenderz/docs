The default configuration from Runcam has the VRX in a locked state that can prevent Input/Output from the OTG. Follow the guide below (1), or watch Joshua Bardwell's Video (2) to unlock it.

1. https://docs.openipc.org/hardware/runcam/vrx/recoverbadflash/

2. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ayw_D18QXGk
